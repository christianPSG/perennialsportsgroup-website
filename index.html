Our Mission & Vision

Based in Austin, Texas, Perennial Sports Group is a full-service management firm that employs advanced data analytics and predictive modeling to provide comprehensive management and representation to elite athletes navigating college athletics. Advanced analytical methods and AI-derived insights provide PSG with cutting-edge granular, player-level models, projections, and comparisons to leverage NIL and revenue-sharing opportunities, offer data-driven guidance, and drive long-term success.

By harnessing advanced data analytics and cutting-edge predictive modeling, Perennial Sports Group delivers actionable insights into optimizing athlete performance, enhancing NIL strategies, navigating the transfer portal, and shaping career success in the evolving college athletics landscape.

This technology-focused approach enables our agents to provide college athletes with bespoke, evidence-based management and representation that empowers our athletes to find their best fit, unlock their true potential, maximize their value, and take ownership of their athletic career.

Meet the Founders

Perennial Sports Group was founded by two former D1-Athletes, individuals with data analytics and legal backgrounds, to guide and prepare athletes at each stage of their athletic careers.

Christian Berry +

Christian Berry competed as a varsity athlete at Princeton University where he majored in Operations Research and Engineering. Over his four years at Princeton, Christian had the opportunity to learn under professional sports executives to develop an understanding how leagues utilize analytics to derive insight for player-contract negotiation, draft selection, and roster construction analysis. Following graduation, Christian worked across firms on Wall Street developing analytical models for financial institutions before moving down to Austin, Texas to found PSG.

Braxton Wilks +

Braxton Wilks, JD competed in collegiate athletics as a college baseball player at Kansas State University, McLennan (Waco, TX), Abilene Christian University, where he graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Accounting, and Virginia Commonwealth University, where he pursued a Master’s of Science in Finance. After hanging up his cleats, Braxton enrolled at Baylor University School of Law, where he received his juris doctorate (J.D.) in 2021. Before founding PSG, Braxton worked for two years as a Corporate, Mergers and Acquisitions attorney at Norton Rose Fulbright LLP in Houston, Texas, and two years as a Wealth Preservation attorney at Winstead PC in Austin, Texas.
